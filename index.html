RISE Strategy

Intel launches its RISE (Responsible, Inclusive, Sustainable, Enabling) strategy and 2030 goals to drive climate action, water stewardship, and waste reduction.

  • Organizes sustainability work into clear pillars and targets.

2023

Renewable Electricity

The company achieves 99% renewable electricity usage worldwide, helping to lower carbon emissions and driving progress toward long-term sustainability goals.

  • Concrete progress milestone (not just a promise).
  • Directly supports emissions reduction goals.
  • Shows momentum toward future targets.

2024

Sustainability Summit

Intel hosts its first Sustainability Summit, uniting suppliers, government officials, and industry leaders to collaborate on next-generation sustainable semiconductor manufacturing.

  • Signals supply chain + partner alignment.
  • Moves sustainability beyond internal operations.
  • Focuses on scaling solutions across the industry.
